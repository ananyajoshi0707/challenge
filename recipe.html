Gingerbread Cookies

A Festive December Treat!

Ingredients

  • 3 cups all-purpose flour
  • 1/2 teaspoon salt
  • 1 teaspoon baking soda
  • 2 teaspoons ground ginger
  • 1 teaspoon cinnamon
  • 1/2 teaspoon ground cloves
  • 1/2 cup unsalted butter, softened
  • 1/2 cup brown sugar
  • 1/2 cup molasses
  • 1 egg
  • 1 teaspoon vanilla extract

Instructions

  1. In a bowl, whisk together flour, salt, baking soda, ginger, cinnamon, and cloves.
  2. In another bowl, cream together butter and sugar until fluffy.
  3. Mix in molasses, egg, and vanilla extract.
  4. Gradually add dry ingredients to the wet mixture, forming a dough.
  5. Chill the dough for at least 1 hour.
  6. Preheat oven to 350°F (175°C) and roll out the dough on a floured surface.
  7. Cut into festive shapes and bake for 8-10 minutes.
  8. Cool completely before decorating as desired.
